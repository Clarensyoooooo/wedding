Our Story

Our Go-With-The-Flow
Love Story

Approximately eight years ago, in the lobby of FAITH, Leomar first noticed Eli because of her loud voice and laughter—though he didn’t even know her name yet. Every time he saw her around campus, he found himself drawn to her cheerful and lively personality.

Fast forward to the time of the pandemic. One chill night, while drinking with his friends, Leomar randomly saw Eli’s Facebook profile in his suggested friends. With a bit of courage from the alcohol, he immediately sent her a friend request. In Eli’s POV, she was usually intentional when accepting friends online. But at that time, something felt different—Leomar’s request caught her attention. She stalked his profile, checked his background, and found out they came from the same college and accepted him. Despite considering this as unusual behavior of her, she tried to accept him because she had a thought of “she might be missing out on being too protective of herself.”

As in... right after, Leomar immediately sent a “Hi.” Eli was a bit hesitant during their “getting to know” stage, but Leomar reassured her that their conversations didn’t have to lead to anything romantic—no pressure, just go with the flow. At that time, neither of them found themselves at their best, as they were dealing with broken souls and personal battles. Still, through each other, they slowly built a genuine connection and companionship.

They went through the getting-to-know-you stage through shared simple, random adventures together. But it wasn’t as smooth, as they encountered multiple goodbyes for choosing self-reconnection.

Despite all the goodbyes and rejections, Leomar never stopped hoping they would find their way back to each other. After taking time and self-reflection, they eventually both took the risk and chose to stay—to root for one another. No proposal happened, they simply realized they had found a home in each other.

Five years later, they decided to tie the knot.
